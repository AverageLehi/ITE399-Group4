PHINMA University of Pangasinan Dagupan Campus (UPANG), located in Dagupan City and is a private, non-sectarian institution under the PHINMA Education network, known for providing affordable, career-focused education and high licensure exam passing rates. It offers senior high school, undergraduate, and graduate programs with a strong emphasis on employability and a 50-75% tuition discount via the "Hawak Kamay" scholarship.

COURSES OFFERED:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing
  • Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Architecture
  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology
